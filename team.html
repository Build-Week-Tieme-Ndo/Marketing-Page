Our Team

The team at Tieme-Ndo has been hard at work perfecting the most robust farm management app for Ghana farmers. This app is geared towards helping farmers to be more profitable as well as use the latest technology to boost crop yields and better track loan payments. Our team is standing by ready to address any issues that you may have when accessing the Tieme-Ndo app. Feel free to reach out through our online contact form if you'd like to learn more about Tieme-Ndo or if you have any additional features that you're interested in us adding.
